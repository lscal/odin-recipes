Pizza rolls

East meets West!

Ingredients

  • Eggroll wrappers
  • Your choice of cheese
  • Pizza sauce
  • Vegetable oil
    •

    Simple, tasty, and easy to make!

    Instructions

    1. Brush light amount of oil onto parchment paper over baking sheet
    2. Lay eggroll wrappers out onto table or other surface
    3. Pour on a spoonful of sauce, and spread evenly in the center
    4. Toss on a handful of cheese
    5. Roll eggroll wrap, and then place onto baking sheet
    6. Cook in oven for around 10-15 minutes
    7. Take out of the oven once brown and crispy, let cool for 10 minutes

    Tips

  • Be sure to use parchment paper! Foil will cause the rolls to stick!
  • Be innovative! These can become your very own by adding just about any ingredient!
